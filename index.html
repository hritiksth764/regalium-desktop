Our Roots

Helmed by Ravi Machani, the Machani Group is a 100-year-old family business with a rich legacy.

We are passionate advocates for the preservation of personal and cultural heritage — defining how future generations will live and grow.

We find our genesis in the values and heritage that we are rooted in. Their retrospective exploration reveals a holistic perspective of the entity we aim to be — and the one we are actively shaping ourselves into.

As a collective network, we work with, and across various domains that contribute to changing the landscape of India’s future — be it defence, robotics, hospitality, textile, publishing, future-forward tech like AI, or even gaming.
