Luxury Retail

At Regalium, we are establishing the 21st-century, holistic luxury retail experience. Discover a retail experience unlike any other in India, where the world’s finest brands meet a thoughtfully curated selection of diverse, high-quality offerings. Here, luxury is redefined through exceptional craftsmanship, innovative expressions, and impeccable service. Seamlessly blending premium customer care with cutting-edge technology, this exclusive space invites members to explore a world of unparalleled elegance, where every detail is designed to elevate your shopping journey.

An Unmatched Luxury - Retail Landscape

Regalium's open-format retail experience breaks free from the confines of India’s traditional boxed-in layout that fails to address the sentiments evoked in shoppers and offers a dynamic and expansive alternative. We create a sense of freedom and discovery, replacing the monotony of traditional spaces with a vibrant, inviting environment.

By prioritising the consumer's needs, this format enriches the joy of shopping, making it an experience that goes beyond mere transactions to deliver lasting satisfaction. Through innovation in retail tech, Regalium will focus on hyper personalisation, easy provenance verification for limited edition products, and provide expert consulting on several fronts such as styling, buying, tax planning, seamless payment options and more.

An Encompassing Scape

As a collective, we are continually inspired by the architectural identity of the region that shaped us. In our pursuit to mirror the characteristic of expansive colonnades — a feature distinctive to South India — we look to repetitive fins and adjusted proportions of openings. And so, we capture the essence of traditional architecture while seamlessly integrating contemporary design elements.
