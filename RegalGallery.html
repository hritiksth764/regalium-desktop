The Regal Gallery

This double-storied, naturally lit space is airy, grand, and dynamic — it is designed to host events, exhibitions, shows, and more. It is designed to ensure the highest level of audio-visual experience — integrated with end-to-end technology to do so.

Bearing in mind the ever-evolving nature of art, artists, and enthusiasts, we cater to experiences that traverse into the digital verse — as a gallery space of the future, the Regal Gallery is designed to take on digital showcases, as well.

Digital galleries are an imperative part of the future, and create opportunities at Regalium for the imagination of our community to continually evolve and expand. With our digital activations — across events and experiences — we serve a curious community with the most faultless amenities.

Enabled with innovative technology to facilitate immersive experiences — this space features sensors attached to walls that can recognise member’s cards for frictionless virtual purchases, 360° cameras that record events live to share with Regalium members at home or to VR headsets, and interactive projected screens and audio guides to enhance the educational immersion of the experience.

Regal Boulevard

Running along Regalium’s South facade on the ground level, the Regal Boulevard is a dynamic outdoor space. With lush greenery and modular seating options, the Boulevard is where users can enjoy an extension of Regalium’s luxury interiors as well as access evolving pop-ups and activities.

The Regal Boulevard’s luxurious plug- and-play capabilities — for hosting events like live performances, exhibitions, product launches, conferences, and film productions — is a welcome addition to the city of Bengaluru.

Executive Education

Effective and impactful leadership holds the power to transform the way that businesses, organisations, and the world at large, function. Our vision for a time in the future, where we are able to draw the most from this leadership, takes shape at our Executive Education space. This space signifies the highest point, aligning with the idea of reaching the pinnacle, in life and in executive leadership. It is designed and developed to be one-of-its-kind multi-use spaces to cater top executives from around the globe. Here, executives are able to train, network, and further nurture their ideas, becoming an all-encompassing space. Equipped with latest tech, ample space for private workshops and discussions, multiple hospitality features, our Executive Education will be the first physical space for individuals to gain imperative knowledge, tools, and actionable methods to be a version of themselves that caters to a larger good — via robust training and learning.
